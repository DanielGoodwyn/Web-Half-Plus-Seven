About

Ever wonder if a potential crush is too young or too old for you to date while maintaining the general approval of society?

Use this app to calculate the socially acceptable age disparity range for potential relationships.

Formula to find the lower limit of the age range:

(your age/2)+7

Formula to find the upper limit of the age range:

(your age-7)*2

This app is obviously useless to anyone under 14.
